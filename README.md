## About Parquet

Parquet is a free, open-source study companion for Microsoft Fabric certifications. Unlike expensive exam prep platforms that charge hundreds of dollars for expert-curated question banks, Parquet provides accessible practice materials to anyone preparing for DP-600, DP-700, or DP-800 exams.

### How Parquet is Different

Most certification prep sites sell expert-written questions behind paywalls. Parquet takes a different approach:

- **100% Free** — No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no paywalls
- **Open Source** — Contribute, improve, and customize the content
- **AI-Generated Questions** — Questions are generated by AI to provide comprehensive coverage of exam topics

### A Note on AI-Generated Content

The questions in Parquet are AI-generated, not written by certified experts. While AI can synthesize information from official documentation and exam objectives effectively, it may occasionally produce inaccuracies or oversimplified explanations.

**We recommend using Parquet as:**
- A supplement to official Microsoft learning paths
- A way to identify knowledge gaps across all exam topics
- Practice material before investing in paid expert question banks
- A starting point for deeper study of each topic

**Please cross-reference important concepts with:**
- Official Microsoft documentation
- Microsoft Learn modules
- Your real-world experience with Fabric

### The Goal

Parquet exists because exam prep shouldn't cost more than the exam itself. Whether you're on a tight budget, exploring a certification before committing, or just want more practice — this is for you.

*Use it. Verify what you learn. Improve it. And pass your exam.*
